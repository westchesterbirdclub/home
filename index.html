Frequently Asked Questions

What does my donation support?

Donations support educational programs, bird conservation, habitat protection, guest speakers, community events, field trips, research, and volunteer activities.

Is my donation secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and process all payments through our secure Stripe portal. We do not store your credit card information on our servers.

Can I become a member?

Absolutely! The West Chester Bird Club welcomes bird enthusiasts of all skill levels. Membership gives you access to special events and regular updates.

Can I volunteer?

Yes, volunteers are the lifeblood of our organization. Opportunities include leading field trips, assisting with educational programs, and participating in habitat restoration.

Is my donation tax deductible?

Yes, West Chester Bird Club is a recognized nonprofit organization. Your donations are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law.
