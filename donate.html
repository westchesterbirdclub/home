Why Your Support Matters

Since 1910, the West Chester Bird Club has relied entirely on the passion and generosity of our community. As a fully volunteer-driven organization, we ensure that every dollar you give goes directly toward fulfilling our mission.

Habitat Conservation

Funding the restoration of local wetlands, forests, and critical nesting grounds to support native and migratory species across Pennsylvania.

Youth & Community Education

Providing binoculars, field guides, and expert speakers for local school programs and free public community bird walks.

Research & Citizen Science

Supporting local ornithological studies, bird-banding stations, and coordinating our annual species census counts.

Our Commitment to Transparency

We believe in complete financial transparency. We are a registered nonprofit organization, and your donations are strictly used for mission-driven activities. We never sell your personal information.
